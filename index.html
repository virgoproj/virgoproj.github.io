Virgo Project

An IDE designed for compiling any language, to any format, any processor, on any computer. You just need to download the Virgo into browser and you have client-side compiler chain ported to JS.

Open Source & Free

Virgo is an open-source and free software. Just open a browser to virgoproj.github.io and you are ready to go!

Get Source

Portable

Virgo has only 2 prerequisites, computer and browser! No spending hundreds of MBs with frameworks anymore...

P.S.: Virgo downloads its modules for processor, format, platform and language.

Client-Sided

Virgo compiles apps and projects client-side. So your source code stays close unless you decide to make it open-source, like Virgo itself.
